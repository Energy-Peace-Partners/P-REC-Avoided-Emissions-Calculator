Methodological Note: P-RECs are energy attribute certificates, not carbon offsets. Avoided emissions should be reported separately from Scope 2 market-based emissions and disclosed with baseline assumptions.

Assumptions & Calculation Details

This tool applies a simplified buyer-side avoided emissions methodology using user-entered baseline shares, baseline emission factors, delivery assumptions, displacement assumptions, and a conservative adjustment factor.

Baseline emissions are estimated as a weighted average of the selected existing off-grid energy sources.

Estimated avoided emissions = P-RECs Purchased × Delivery Factor × Displacement Factor × Conservative Factor × (Baseline Emission Factor − Project Emission Factor)

Results are indicative only and should be disclosed together with the assumptions used.

Data Sources & References:
